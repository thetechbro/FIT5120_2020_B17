Older people make a considerable proportion of Australia’s population. With a steeper increase in their community across Australia, there is a dire need to comprehend the difficulties faced by the elderly.  In Melbourne, the majority of elderly people are using residential aged care or home services for their needs. Choosing the right aged care service, which will mainly focus on their difficulties and help them feel safe and secure, proves to be the toughest decision. Hence, helping the elderly in finding the appropriate aged care forms a vital part of our website for those settled in Melbourne.   

In order to help elderly manage their expenses, our website analysis the average montly expenditure of different age groups. We set up some questions, on answering those questions user can know how much their aged group can save on some items. Users can also see a pie chart which shows the consumption pattern of different aged groups.
